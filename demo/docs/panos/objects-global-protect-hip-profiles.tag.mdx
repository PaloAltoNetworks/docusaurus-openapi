Manages HIP profiles which are a collection of HIP objects. A HIP profile is a collection of HIP objects that are evaluated together
either for monitoring or for security policy enforcement. Using HIP for policy enforcement allows you to ensure
that the hosts and endpoints accessing your critical resources are adequately maintained and in adherence with your security standards
before they are allowed access to your network resources.
